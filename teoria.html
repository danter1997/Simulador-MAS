Diagrama de cuerpo libre: \begin{align} \sum F_m &= m\ddot{x} \\\\ T - mg &= m\ddot{x} \ \ \ \ \ (1) \end{align}

Ecuación de movimiento angular de la polea: \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \sum M_o &= I \alpha \\ T R - F k R &= I \alpha \\ T R - \bigl(k (x + x_o) R \bigr) &= I \ddot{\theta} \ \ \ \ \ \ \ (2) \end{aligned} \end{equation}

Cuando el sistema está en equilibrio se presenta la siguiente igualdad: \[ mgR = kx_oR \] \begin{aligned} \theta &= \frac{x}{R} \\ \ddot{\theta} &= \frac{\ddot{x}}{R} \end{aligned}

Fuerzas y torques:

El momento de inercia de la polea es: \begin{equation} \frac{1}{2} M r^2 \end{equation} Por lo cual la ecuación 2 queda: \begin{aligned} T R - (k (x + x_o) R) &= I \ddot{\theta} \\ R (m g + m \ddot{x}) - k x R - m g R &= \frac{1}{2} M R^\cancel{2} \left(\frac{\ddot{x}}{\cancel{R}}\right) \\ \cancel{R}(\cancel{mg} + m\ddot{x} - kx - \cancel{mg}) &= \frac{1}{2}M\cancel{R} \ddot{x} \\ -m\ddot{x} - kx &= \frac{1}{2}M\ddot{x} \\ \frac{1}{2}M\ddot{x} + m\ddot{x} + kx &= 0 \\ \ddot{x}\left(\frac{1}{2}M + m \right) &= -kx \\ \ddot{x} &= \frac{-k}{M/2 + m}x \\ \omega &= \sqrt{\frac{k}{M/2 + m}} \end{aligned}

Energías:

\begin{aligned} E &= K + K_R + U = constante\\\\ E &= \frac{1}{2}m \dot{x}^2 + \frac{1}{2}IW^2 + \frac{1}{2}kx^2 \\\\ E &= \frac{1}{2}m \dot{x}^2 + \frac{1}{2}IW^2 + \frac{1}{2}kx^2 \\\\ E &= \frac{1}{2}m \dot{x}^2 + \frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{1}{2}M\cancel{R^2} \right) \left(\frac{1}{\cancel{R}}\dot{x} \right)^\cancel{2} + \frac{1}{2}kx^2 \\\\ E &= \frac{1}{2}m \dot{x}^2 + \frac{1}{4}M\dot{x}^2 + \frac{1}{2}kx^2 \\\\ E &= \frac{1}{2}\left(m + \frac{M}{2}\right)\dot{x}^2 + \frac{1}{2}kx^2 \\\\ \frac{dE}{dt} &= 0 = \left(m + \frac{M}{2} \right) \dot{x}\ddot{x} + kx\dot{x} \\\\ &\left[ \left(m + \frac{M}{2} \right) \ddot{x} + kx \right]\dot{x} = 0 \rightarrow \dot{x} \neq 0 \\ \end{aligned}

\begin{aligned} &\left(m + \frac{M}{2} \right) \ddot{x} + kx = 0 \\\\ &\ddot{x} + \frac{k}{m + \frac{M}{2}}x = 0 \\\\ & \ddot{x} = \frac{-k}{M/2 + m}x \\\\ & \omega = \sqrt{\frac{k}{M/2 + m}} \end{aligned}
